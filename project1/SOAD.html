Sytem of a Down

System of a Down (often abbreviated as SOAD or simply System) is an Armenian-American rock band formed in Glendale, California, in 1994. The band consists of vocalist Serj Tankian, guitarist and vocalist Daron Malakian, bassist Shavo Odadjian, and drummer John Dolmayan. They are known for their unique sound that blends elements of metal, punk, and traditional Armenian music, as well as their politically charged lyrics addressing issues such as war.
